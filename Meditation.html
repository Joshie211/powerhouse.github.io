Meditation~

Meditation is a practice where an individual uses a technique to train attention, awareness and achieve a mentally clear, emotionally calm and stable state. Meditation trains the brain to achieve sustained focus, and to return to that focus when negative thinking, emotion, and physical sensations intrude-which happens a lot when you feel stressed and anxious.

For PowerHouse, it would be a Guided Meditation. Guided Meditation is where You form mental images of places or situations you find relaxing. You try to use as many senses as possible and may be led through this process by a guide of teachers.
